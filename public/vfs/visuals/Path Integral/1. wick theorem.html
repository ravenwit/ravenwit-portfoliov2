Vacuum State Topology

Evaluating the Vacuum Expectation Value (VEV) of N=6 distinct fields:

By Wick's theorem, the time-ordered product expands into normal-ordered sums. However, because we evaluate the vacuum state ⟨0|...|0⟩, any normal-ordered term with uncontracted operators annihilates to zero.

Only the fully contracted permutations survive. By pairing fields recursively, exactly (N-1)!! = 15 valid Feynman propagators emerge.
